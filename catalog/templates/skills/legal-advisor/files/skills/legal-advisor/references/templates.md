## Email Marketing Compliance

### CAN-SPAM Requirements

```markdown
Every commercial email must include:
1. Accurate "From" header identifying the sender
2. Subject line that reflects the content
3. Clear identification as an advertisement (if applicable)
4. Physical postal address of the sender
5. Opt-out mechanism (must be processed within 10 business days)
6. No purchased or harvested email lists
```

### CASL Requirements (Canada)

```markdown
1. Express consent required before sending (implied consent in limited cases)
2. Sender identification (name, contact information)
3. Physical mailing address
4. Unsubscribe mechanism (must be processed within 10 business days)
5. Records of consent (when obtained, method, purpose disclosed)
```

## COPPA Compliance Addendum

### Required Elements

```markdown
# Children's Privacy Notice

## Age Screening
- Implement age gate before data collection
- Neutral age screening (don't encourage lying)

## Parental Consent
- Verifiable parental consent method:
  - Signed consent form (mail/fax/email scan)
  - Credit card or other payment verification
  - Video call verification
  - Government ID verification
  - Knowledge-based authentication

## Data Minimization
- Collect only what's necessary for the activity
- No behavioral advertising to children
- No conditioning participation on unnecessary data

## Parental Rights
- View child's personal information
- Delete child's personal information
- Refuse further collection
- Contact method for exercising rights

## Operator Obligations
- Direct notice to parents
- Post privacy policy
- Maintain confidentiality and security
- Retain data only as long as necessary
- FTC Safe Harbor program participation (optional)
```
